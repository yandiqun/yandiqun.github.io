Short Bio.

Xianliang Jiang is a Lecturer with the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Ningbo University, Ningbo, China since 2016. He received a B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology (Full-time Adult Higher Education) from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2009 , a M.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology under the supervision of Prof. Guang Jin from Ningbo University in 2012, and a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology under the supervision of Prof. Jiangang Yang from Zhejiang University in 2016, respectively. His research interests include: Congestion Control Algorithms in Cellular Networks and Datacenter, Machine Learning in Network Protocol Design, Internet of Things System, Network Security.
